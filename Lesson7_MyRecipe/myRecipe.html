Perfect Peanut Butter Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of your favorite creamy peanut butter
  • 1 cup of white granulated sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup of chocolate chips (optional)
  • 1 splash of vanilla extract (optional)

Instructions

Preheat oven to 325.

Mix peanut butter, sugar, and egg.

Add optional extras.

Roll into one inch diameter balls.

Place dough balls on ungreased baking sheet.

Bake until golden brown and edges begin to crack.

Allow to cool, and enjoy.
